Chicken Briyani

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant, spiced rice dish layered with marinated chicken, cooked with aromatic spices like saffron, cardamom, and cloves. This flavorful and hearty dish is a beloved staple in South Asian cuisine, often garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs.

Rs.249

Chicken Pasta

Chicken pasta is a savory dish featuring tender chicken pieces and al dente pasta, tossed in a creamy or tomato-based sauce. It's often enhanced with garlic, herbs, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese for a flavorful, comforting meal.

Rs.149

Set Dosa

Set dosa is a soft, spongy South Indian pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. Typically served in a stack of three, it is accompanied by sambar and chutney, offering a mild, fluffy contrast to the crispy traditional dosa.

Rs.99
